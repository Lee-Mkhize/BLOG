About:

Artist: Smino

Release date: 23 December 2019

Genres: hip Hop/Rap, Seasonal

Label: Zero Fatigue/Downtown/Interscope Records

Smino finished off a busy 2019 full of features (from collabs with SiR to his collective Pivot Gang) to drop this holiday themed two-pack. He copped assistance from producer Monte Booker and Masego, and dropped the EP on December 22, 2019 after teasing fans with videos of him bumping the tracks on Twitter and Instagram.
